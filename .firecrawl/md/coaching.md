# Coaching Services

**Change-Well offers specialized coaching services designed to support personal growth, performance development, and conflict resolution. Coaching services focus on building practical skills that help individuals improve decision-making, communication, and resilience in both personal and professional settings.**

## Performance Coaching

## ![](https://www.change-well.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/1128/2025/10/Dr.-Kyle-Stull-Performance-Coach--300x300.jpeg)Dr. Kyle Stull, MA, DHSc

> **Performance Coach**
>
> Introductory Pricing starting at $90.00 a session, [click here for special packages](https://www.change-well.com/performance-coaching-pricing/)
>
> To schedule: [972-427-2050](tel:972.427.2050) or [liz@change-well.com](mailto:liz@change-well.com)

Dr. Kyle Stull is a Doctor of Health Science with a concentration in behavior. He brings more than twenty years of experience in athletic performance, injury prevention, and rehabilitation. Through working with both competitive and everyday athletes, he observed that confidence—not physical ability—often becomes the deciding factor in performance and recovery. This realization inspired him to expand his expertise into the psychology of sport and performance, earning a postgraduate certificate in sports psychology coaching.

Kyle has worked with athletes in high-pressure sports, such as rodeo and baseball, and now partners with clients to strengthen the mental skills that support focus, resilience, and overall well-being. His approach integrates science, practical experience, and compassion, helping people cultivate the confidence to thrive not only in competition but also in everyday life.

Dr. Diann Sanford has worked those seeking conflict resolution in complex personal, family and business issues for several years. She has often been referred by the court and sometimes ordered by the court to serve couples or families for difficult parental alienation cases. She has assisted attorneys, medical professionals, business owners, pastors, corporate leaders and others with more suitable and effective outcomes when confronted with tough decisions and heavy demands. After establishing clear objectives upfront, Dr. Sanford can skillfully help you open doors you thought were closed or sometimes help you create an entirely new door from which to emerge. Her methods are direct, powerful, collaborative and compassionate.

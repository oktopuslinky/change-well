# Dr. Diann Sanford, licensed clinical psychologist, and her team welcomes you…

## We are a group of licensed clinical psychologists and therapists that serve clients and area physicians in and around Plano, Austin and the entire state of Texas.

We look at the “total person” in order to secure an accurate diagnosis and meaningful treatment plan. We all work hard to create a therapeutic atmosphere that promotes measurable growth and positive change!

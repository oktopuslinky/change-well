# Crisis Helplines

**In case you need immediate help:** If ever you are feeling hopeless, suicidal, or simply in need of speaking with someone, there are numerous free and anonymous hotlines with trained operators available.

**Suicide Prevention / Depression Support**

- US: [Hopeline (Suicide Prevention)](http://www.hopeline.com/): 1-800-SUICIDE (1-800-784-2433)
- US: [Suicide Hotline](http://www.suicidepreventionlifeline.org/): 1-800-273-Talk (1-800-8255)
- UK: [Samaritans (Nat’l and local)](http://www.samaritans.org/): 08457 90 90 90 or [jo@samaritans.org](mailto:jo@samaritans.org)
- CAN: [Suicide Prevention Canada](http://suicideprevention.ca/thinking-about-suicide/find-a-crisis-centre/)
- AUS: [Lifeline Australia](http://www.lifeline.org.au/) Helpline 13 11 14

**Substance Abuse / Alcoholism**

- US: National Drug Information Treatment and Referral Hotline: 1-800-662-HELP (4357)
- US: Alcohol Abuse and Crisis Intervention: 1-800-234-0246
- US: National Cocaine Hotline: 1-800-COCAINE (262-2463)
- US: Al-Anon/Alateen Hotline (for friends and family) 1-800-344-2666
- UK: [Drinkline: Nat’l. Alcoholism Helpline](http://www.rcpsych.ac.uk/mentalhealthinformation/mentalhealthproblems/alcoholanddrugs/alcoholourfavouritedrug.aspx): 0800 917 8282
- AUS: Drug & Alcohol Helpline 1800 198 024
- CAN: Drug & Alcohol Helpline 1-800-565-8603

**Rape, Abuse & Domestic Violence**

- US: [Childhelp (Child Abuse)](http://www.childhelp.org/about/programs-and-services/childhelp-national-child-abuse-hotline-1-800-4-a-child): 1-800-4ACHILD (1-800-422-4453)
- US: [Nat’l. Domestic Violence Hotline](http://www.ndvh.org/): 1-800-799-SAFE (7233)
- US: [RAINN Nat’l. Sexual Assault Hotline](http://www.rainn.org/): 1-800-656-HOPE (4673)
- US: [Nat’l. Center on Elder Abuse](https://ncea.acl.gov/): 1-800-677-1116
- US: National Runaway Switchboard: 1-800-621-4000
- UK: [Women’s Aid Domestic Violence Helpline](http://www.womensaid.org.uk/): 0808 2000 247

**Gay / Lesbian Issues**

- US: [GLBT National Help Center](http://www.glbthotline.org/) 1-888-843-4564
- UK: [Galop – LGBT Abuse](http://www.galop.org.uk/) Helpline 0800 999 5428
- CAN: [PFLAG Canada](http://www.glbthotline.org/) Helpline 1-888-530-6777
- AUS: QLIFE Australia Helpline 1800 184 527

**Pregnancy / Sexual Health**

- US: [Planned Parenthood](http://www.plannedparenthood.org/): 1-800-230-PLAN (7526)
- US: [Nat’l. STD Hotline](http://www.thebody.com/hotlines/national.html): 1-800-227-8922
- US: National AIDS Hotline: 1-800-342-AIDS (2437)
- US: The Teen AIDS Hotline: 1-800-440-TEEN (8336)

**Medical Emergencies**

- US: [Nat’l. Poison Help](http://www.aapcc.org/): 1-800-222-1222

**ADDITIONAL RESOURCES / ORGANIZATIONS**

- [ULifeline](http://www.ulifeline.org/) – A directory of resources available for college students— take a self-assessment test; or find your college psych services center.
- [Alcoholics Anonymous](http://www.aa.org/) (AA) – Alcoholics Anonymous is an informal meeting society for recovering alcoholics whose primary purpose is to stay sober and help other alcoholics achieve sobriety.
- [Narcotics Anonymous](http://www.na.org/) (NA) – Narcotics Anonymous similar to AA, is an international, community-based association of recovering addicts.
- [Self-Mutilators Anonymous](http://www.selfmutilatorsanonymous.org/) – A community-based association for individuals struggling with self-mutilation.

**INFORMATIONAL RESOURCES**

- [National Institute of Mental Health](http://www.nimh.nih.gov/health/topics/) – Extensive information on a variety of mental health topics.
- [National Institute of Health](http://health.nih.gov/) – Extensive information on all health related topics.
- [MedlinePlus](http://medlineplus.gov/) – Extensive information of different drugs and treatment options.

_If you think you have a medical emergency **please call your doctor or 911 immediately.**_

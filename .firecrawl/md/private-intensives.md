# Customized Couples Therapy Intensives

> ![](https://www.change-well.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/1128/2020/02/Couples-Field-Crop-300x193.jpg)“Individuals are asked to take a hard look “inward”, and not come to therapy “to fix” his/her partner. There are no losers in my couples therapy, if there is, I have not done my job. I work had to establish a win-win, where each individual has a clear voice and all goals are mutually agreed upon.”
>
> ###### – Dr. Diann Sanford, Psy.D, LPC

_**Couples therapy intensives provide focused, extended sessions designed to help partners address challenges and make meaningful progress in a shorter period of time. These sessions allow couples to work more deeply on relationship concerns than traditional weekly therapy.**_

## **Is a Private Intensive right for us?**

**Crisis/Critical**

If you and your partner are going through a particularly difficult time, you’re not alone. In situations involving things like infidelity, addiction, or other complex challenges, it may take more than one intensive session to begin rebuilding and moving forward. After an initial conversation and assessment, we’ll create a plan that fits your specific situation, which may also include a few individual sessions for additional support.

**Moderate**

If you and your partner are feeling stuck in one or two key areas, a single intensive session is often a strong starting point. Some couples may benefit from a follow-up session, but many begin to see meaningful progress during this focused time together.

**Enrichment**

If your relationship is in a good place, but you’re looking to feel more connected or aligned, an intensive can be a meaningful investment. Couples in this stage often use the experience to strengthen their bond and explore new ways of growing together.

### **Agenda for an Intensive**

_As part of the intensive experience, you and your partner will move through a structured process designed to support meaningful progress and lasting change. You can expect the following:_

**In Advance:**

- Complete confidential assessments and intake paperwork
- Begin with a shared commitment to your relationship and the intensive process

Therapeutic sessions consisting of 4 Parts, based on **[the Gottman Method](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AKTyPgwfPgg)** and thoughtfully tailored to your unique needs:

**Foundational Skills**

- Establish a strong foundation for individual and relationship health
- Build communication skills by working through real situations in real time
- Begin developing new, healthier ways of relating to one another

**Heavy Lifting _(this portion is more in-depth in private intensives than in semi-private retreats)_**

- Address areas where you feel stuck
- Work through patterns and begin the process of repair, forgiveness, and letting go

_A one-hour lunch break is typically included._

**Action**

- Create a clear and mutually agreed-upon path forward
- Identify supports, obstacles, and resources needed
- Define specific behaviors and steps to continue progress

**Intentional Restoration**

- Strengthen commitment to one another and the relationship
- Build a shared vision with new behaviors and a deeper connection
- Make space for positive experiences, reflection, and growth

_Before leaving, we will develop a plan together to help you maintain your progress, and you will be invited to complete a brief post-assessment._

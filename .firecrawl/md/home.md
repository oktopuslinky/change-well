# Change-Well \| Therapy and Psychological Testing

![](https://www.change-well.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/1128/2023/02/067_Sanford_-Diann2-1-e1735333690542-272x300.jpg)If you are looking for guidance with transitions, anxiety, trauma, fears, depression, relationships, or other issues, our team is ready to help. It takes courage to ask for help. The good news is that asking for assistance gets easier with a little practice.

At Change-Well, we provide therapy and psychological evaluation services in Plano, Texas. Our licensed clinicians work with individuals, couples, and families seeking clarity, healing, and growth. We offer trauma-informed therapy for anxiety, depression, and trauma, along with comprehensive psychological testing and personalized treatment planning in a supportive, professional setting.

* * *

_“With kindness and enthusiasm we will work with you to create the changes and the life you earnestly desire. Through the assimilation of proven therapies we will develop agreed upon goals, and work towards better balance and improved health/vitality for total wellness. This will allow you to discover new energies, as well as experience a more productive way of being, happier relationships, and closer connections with those you love.” —Dr. Diann Sanford_

* * *

## How We Can Support You

_**Individual Therapy**_ We provide therapy for anxiety, trauma, depression, life transitions, and personal growth. Our clinicians use evidence-based and trauma-informed approaches tailored to each client’s needs. Sessions are available in person in Plano and through secure telehealth.

_**[Couples Counseling](https://www.change-well.com/couples-therapy/)**_ We support couples navigating communication challenges, conflict, trust concerns, and life changes. Our goal is to help partners strengthen connections, build understanding, and develop healthier patterns within the relationship.

_**Psychological Evaluation**_ We offer comprehensive psychological evaluations to provide clarity and direction for concerns such as ADHD, learning differences, mood disorders, and diagnostic questions. Evaluations are conducted in Plano, Texas, and include detailed feedback and recommendations.

_**Family Counseling**_ Family counseling helps families navigate communication challenges, conflict, transitions, and relationship concerns. Our clinicians work with family members to strengthen understanding, improve communication, and support healthier dynamics within the family system.

* * *

## Why Choose Change-Well

At Change-Well, we believe trust is at the heart of meaningful care. Our clinicians take the time to truly listen and understand each person’s experience, offering thoughtful, evidence-based support tailored to your needs. We’re committed to creating a space where individuals, couples, and families feel comfortable, respected, and supported as they work toward clarity, stability, and lasting change.

## Why Should I Attend Therapy or Participate in Teletherapy?

Sometimes, having a clearer understanding of your experiences can make all the difference. Psychological evaluations can provide insight into concerns like ADHD, autism, behavioral issues, and mood-related challenges. Our team offers comprehensive testing in Plano, Texas, along with supportive, easy-to-understand feedback to help you move forward with confidence.

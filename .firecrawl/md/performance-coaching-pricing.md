# Performance Coaching Pricing

**Special 2025 Introductory Pricing**

Start your journey toward greater confidence, focus, and resilience with our limited-time introductory offers. Whether you’re a student athlete, an adult competitor, or part of a team, these packages provide an accessible way to experience the benefits of mental performance coaching.

**For Students (Ages 13–19)**

Confidence is often the missing piece between practice and performance. Our 2025 Student Package offers shorter, focused sessions designed to help teens build the mindset, focus, and resilience they need to succeed in both sports and academics—all at a special introductory rate.

**For Adults (20+)**

Performance challenges don’t just happen on the field—they show up in work, competition, and everyday life. Our 2025 Adult Package is designed to help you reset, strengthen resilience, and develop the confidence to perform consistently under pressure, at a special introductory rate.
